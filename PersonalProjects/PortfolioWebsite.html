About this site

Creation

This website was created after I needed a way to show potential employers what my resume could not. I saw a video explaining github pages and what others had done with it and was inspired. I got onto my then empty github account and got to work.

Development

The website was created and worked on for 2 weeks before it was presentable to get my first job. After getting the job the website was abandoned until May 21,2022. Having increased my knowledge on Webdevelopment and having new projects to add to it I came back to update it. This will be a living document that will grow as I grow as a person and as a developer. Will try to provide frequent updates but if things get busy it will be the first to get the axe.
